The easiest way to sell meal preparation with WordPress.

== Description ==

Food To Prep is a complete eCommerce solution for selling meal preparation on WordPress.

= Sell effortlessly =

Regardless of the types of meals you're selling to your customers, Food To Prep gives you the perfect solution to take your meal prep business online, into WordPress and manage your orders from start to finish, effortlessly.

Our hope is to add more support, flexibility and functionality as users start feeding back about the plugin.

= Payment support with PayPal =

In this early version of the plugin, we've provided support using PayPal, allowing you to quickly and easily take credit and debit card payments from your customers meaning that you don't have to handle cash and you can start prepping your orders knowing that you've already been paid.

We'll be adding more payment gateways as requested by our users.

= Built with developers in mind =

Food To Prep was built by developers and we'll be supporting it's extension as it's popularity grows. We'll be keeping the core plugin open source and allowing you to work with it any way you like so that it can best meet your meal prep needs.

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin
2. Go to Meal Prep > Payment Setting and configure your PayPal integration
3. Go to Meals and create meals for your customers to order
4. Your customers will now be able to view your meals at /meal-list
5. Orders placed will be visible through the Meal Prep > Orders link in the WP Dashboard
